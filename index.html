Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for MultiDesk

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use MultiDesk (the "Application") on Windows 11. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information Collection and Use

  • No Personal Data: The Application does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, or phone number).
  • Offline Operation: The Application is designed to function locally on your device. Any data you input into the Application remains on your local storage and is not uploaded to our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Microsoft Store: The Application is distributed through the Microsoft Store. Microsoft may collect certain telemetry and usage data according to its own Privacy Policy to facilitate downloads and updates.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share any data with third-party advertising or analytics companies.

4. Permissions

The Application only requests permissions that are strictly necessary for its core functionality, such as access to local storage for saving your workspace preferences.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within this document or updated on the official Microsoft Store listing.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: MultiDesktop@hotmail.com
