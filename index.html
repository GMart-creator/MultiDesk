Privacy Policy

At MultiDesk, we value your privacy. This policy explains how our application handles information.

Data Collection

MultiDesk is a local productivity tool. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, desktop files, or browsing history to external servers. All workspace configurations and shortcut links are stored locally on your device.

Microsoft Store

As the app is distributed via the Microsoft Store, Microsoft may collect basic telemetry data as outlined in their privacy policy. We do not have access to your personal identity or payment information.

Permissions

The app requires permissions to manage desktop icons and change system wallpapers to provide its core functionality. These actions are performed exclusively on your local machine.

For further inquiries regarding privacy, please contact us at MultiDesktop@hotmail.com.
